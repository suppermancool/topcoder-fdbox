Today, on 2012-04-22, the following persons appeared in front of me, Notary Name, notary public with my offices at 15 Notary Street, in the offices of Attorney Name at 15 Attorney Avenue, whereto I went upon request:

Shareholder One, born on 2014-12-251 Shareholder Address, represented by POA One, born on 2012-01-24, 15 POA Way, acting pursuant to the special power of attorney attached (Annex ./1) and Shareholder Two, born on 2016-01-172 Shareholder Address,

and submitted the following

articles of association

  1. Company name

Shareholder One, born on 2014-12-251 Shareholder Address and Shareholder Two, born on 2016-01-172 Shareholder Address incorporate as shareholders a limited liability company with the name FBDOX Company Inc.

  1. Seat

The legal seat of the company shall be in FBDOX Company Seat.

  1. Object of the company

    1. The object of the company shall be FBDOX Business.

    2. The company may undertake all legal transactions and actions that are necessary or convenient to the achievement of the purpose of the company. The performance of banking transactions is excluded.

  2. Nominal capital, initial contributions

    1. The nominal capital of the company shall be EUR 700.000$.

    2. Shareholder One shall assume an initial contribution in the amount of EUR 400.000$, of which 400.000$ shall be paid in before the registration of the company with the Commercial Register. Shareholder Two shall assume an initial contribution in the amount of EUR 300.000$, of which 300.000$ shall be paid in before the registration of the company with the Commercial Register.

  3. Duration, financial year

    1. The duration of the company shall be perpetual.

    2. The first financial year shall start on the day of incorporation of the company in the commercial register and shall last until the subsequent 31 December. All following financial years shall end on 31 December.

  4. Bodies of the corporation

Bodies of the corporation shall be:

    1. The shareholders' meeting,

    2. The board of directors.

  1. Shareholders' meeting

    1. Shareholders' meetings shall be convoked by the board of directors by registered letter, fax or e-mail to all shareholders, stating the place, day, time and the agenda of the shareholders' meeting, provided that a period of at least two weeks shall lapse between the day the notice is placed in the mail or sent by fax or e-mail and the day of the shareholders' meeting. Each director may convoke a shareholders' meeting. The convocation can also be made by any shareholders.

    2. The shareholders' meeting shall take place at the seat of the company or in any Austrian town that hosts the registered office of a notary public.

    3. The shareholders may be represented in a shareholders' meeting by a proxy duly authorized by a written power of attorney.

    4. If all shareholders are present or properly represented and agree to the adoption of resolutions, then resolutions can be adopted even without compliance with statutory and contractual provisions concerning convocation formalities.

  2. Shareholders' resolutions

    1. Shareholders' resolutions shall be adopted in shareholders' meetings. Apart from shareholders' meetings resolutions may be adopted by circular resolution if mandatory statutory provisions do not provide for another form and if each shareholder takes part in and no shareholder contradicts such circular resolution. The directors shall set up a protocol on each resolution without delay and send it to each shareholder.

    2. In order to constitute a quorum, more than half of the nominal capital has to be represented in a shareholders' meeting. If a shareholders' meeting is not quorate, a second shareholders' meeting has to be convoked with reference thereto without delay. Such shareholders' meeting is restricted to the agenda which has caused the convocation of the first shareholders' meeting. The second shareholders' meeting is quorate irrespective of the nominal capital represented.

    3. Shareholders' resolutions are passed by simple majority of the votes cast if no higher majority is needed pursuant to the statute. Each ten Euros of the nominal capital subscribed for shall grant a vote, fractions of a vote are disregarded. Each shareholder shall have at least one vote.

  3. Board of directors

    1. The company shall have one, two or three directors. If two or three directors are appointed, they shall represent the company jointly or one director shall represent the company together with an authorised representative.

    2. If several directors are appointed, the shareholders may grant sole power of representation to some of them.

    3. The shareholders' meeting may enact rules of procedure which can be supplemented and changed at any time in order to bindingly regulate the activities of the directors and the allocation of duties among several directors.

    4. The directors are obliged to conduct the business of the company in accordance with statutory provisions, this declaration regarding the formation of a company, the rules of procedure as well as the shareholders' resolutions.

    5. Each of the directors shall be responsible for the entire management of the company and shall have the right to get information on, to control and, in case two directors are appointed, to make objections in the sense of sec. 115 (section one hundred and fifteen) of the Austrian Companies Act with regard to all areas of management, even if not being directly responsible for them.

  4. Annual financial statements

    1. The directors shall within five months after the end of each financial year prepare annual financial statements including a management report and notes for the previous financial year pursuant to the provisions of the Austrian Companies Act and, if required by law, have them audited by an independent auditor.

    2. The board of directors shall submit the annual financial statements of the company together with the management report and the audit report by an independent auditor, if any, to the shareholders' meeting for examination and approval pursuant to sec. 35(1)(1) (section thirty-five paragraph one sub-paragraph one) of the Austrian Limited Liability Companies Act without delay.

    3. On the basis of a proposal of the board of directors, the shareholders' meeting shall, after the examination and approval of the annual financial statements, resolve on the use of the annual profit or loss of the year.

  5. Profit participation

The shareholders shall participate in the profits of the company pro rata their shares in the nominal capital of the company.

  1. Transfer of shares

The shares are divisible and transferable.

  1. Notifications

The notifications of the company to the shareholders or of shareholders to other shareholders shall be made by registered mail, fax or e-mail to the latest addresses notified to the company.

  1. Severability clause

If any of the provisions of these articles of association are invalid or if these articles of association contain gaps, this shall not affect the validity of the remaining provisions. The invalid provision shall be deemed replaced by such valid provision which corresponds to the aim of the invalid provision. In case of gaps such provision shall be deemed included that corresponds to the aim of this declaration regarding the formation of the company which would have been included herein if this issue had been thought of from the beginning.

  1. Formal changes

Attorney Name with its business address at 15 Attorney Avenue is empowered and instructed by the shareholders to effect all changes in these articles of association, especially changes in connection with orders of the commercial court, with legally binding effect and to sign applications to the commercial register in connection thereto.

  1. Costs, taxes and duties

All costs, taxes and duties in connection with the establishment and the registration of the company shall be borne by the company up to the maximum amount of EUR 900000000. The costs of formation of the company shall be included as costs in the first financial statements of the company up to the maximum amount as mentioned.

Any number of executed copies of this notarial deed may be issued to the represented parties and its legal successors, as well as to the company itself, in each case upon unilateral request and at the expense of the requesting party.

With regard to this subject matter, this notarial deed was recorded, read out loud to the persons present, expressly approved by these persons as completely corresponding to the true will of the party represented, and signed by hand in front of me, notary public.
